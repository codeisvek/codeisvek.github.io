About

I’m an interaction and UI/UX Designer, developer and student at UC Berkeley. At Berkeley, I help educate and introduce people to hacking and design through Hackers @ Berkeley. I organized and directed design and branding for Cal Hacks, the largest collegiate hackathon of all time. I design stuff through Innovative Design. I like to make stuff and help run both the Supernode student makerspace as well as the Bridges Makerspace. I am also an undergraduate researcher under professor Eric Paulos’ group in the Hybrid Ecologies lab where we explore fabrication and how humans interact with technology.

In my free time, I DJ and produce music. I take pictures and put them on Instagram. I make digital paintings on my iPad. I also write on Medium.

You can reach me through email at david@dtbui.com. Say hi! I am also available for freelance work.
