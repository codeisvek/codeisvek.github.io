hey, i'm david bui.

I’m a 19 year old student currently studying at UC Berkeley. At Berkeley, I help educate and introduce people to hacking and design through Hackers @ Berkeley. I organized and lead design and branding for Cal Hacks, the largest collegiate hackathon of all time. I design stuff through Innovative Design. I dance (badly). I am also an undergraduate researcher under professor Eric Paulos’ group in the Tactical Hybrid Ecologies lab.

In my free time, I DJ and produce music. I take pictures and put them on Instagram. I make digital paintings on my iPad. I also write on Medium.

You can reach me through email at david@dtbui.com. Say hi! I am also available for freelance work.
