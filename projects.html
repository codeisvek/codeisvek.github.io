The projects.

Just some of the stuff I've done so far.

Cloudchat

Cloudchat was a small web application I designed and developed as a solo project during the CSUA Hackathon.

It is a basic chat room application written in Javascript on the front-end and the back-end using Meteor. This webapp is styled entirely using CSS3 styles.

CSUAHack Site

A quick website made for the CSUA Hackathon during Fall 2014. The website simply displayed resources for the Hackathon, featuring a schedule, submission link, countdown, as well as various other uselful links. In addition to this I designed the promotional flyer for the event, found under "see more."

The site is still up at time of writing.

Hack61A

A really quick website made in several hours for the CS61A Beginner Hackathon (CS61A is the first Compter Science Class at UC Berkeley.) This site displayed basic information for the Hackathon, a countdown, as well as links to useful resources and the information from the several workshops that were held during the event.

Whalr

Whalr was a project that I worked on in a team during CalHacks 2014. Whalr was a webapp that displayed what Berkeley's dining halls served and notified site members of when their favorite foods were available.

I was responsible for doing the design as well as coding up the front end. Although the project was not finished during the hackathon, I finished the design and front end, as well as a small prototype, and have attached screenshots under "see more."

BerkeleyBord

A small mobile webapp that is simply a local bulletin board (specifically catered towards Berkeley Students.) BerkeleyBord allowed Users to post anonymous messages as well as upvote or downvote messages.

BerkeleyBord was made with a small team during the HackJam Hackathon. In this project, I was responsible for making the design assets/mockups as well as coding up the backend.

This hack won "Prettiest App & Best Hating on the East Coast" and for that I got myself a nice Arduino to do hardware hacks on.

RightMindLab

An old project from High School. Right Mind Lab was a small group made up of several contempories and me that did creative services like branding, web design, and videography for local groups. 

I also designed the Logo and the Website, which is based off a free template by LINKGOESHERE

PHS App

An old and unforunately dead project I did while I was in ASB (Associated Student Body) at Pacifica High School. The PHS App was a mobile app that displayed useful things like school news as well as a schedule.

The "App" was made up of a simple wordpress site that with a theme customized to look mobile. In addition to that, I made a icon and splash image which, using the meta tags for iOS webapps, made it seem like a native app. The android app was simply a webview that pointed to the same site.

Pacifica NHS

Another old website done in High School. This one was a site made for my school's National Honor Society chapter. 

Because the people running the club wanted the site to run on Weebly, this site is made up of a custom weebly theme.
